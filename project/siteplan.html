Honest Kitchen

This name represents the nutritional information about the recipes in the site or uploaded by the user. It means that all the information given by the site is factual, honest, and meant to inform the user.

Site Purpose

This site provides a homepage with meal, breakfast, or dessert recipes that include nutritional information, there's a page where the user can upload their own recipes and get the nutritional information for them, and finally a page that displays all their uploaded recipes with nutritional information.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a breakfast recipe that meets my nutritional needs?
  • How much protein is in my lunch?
  • How can I find the nutritional info for my bread recipe?
