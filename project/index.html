Welcome to Honest Kitchen!

A site in which you can find recipes that meet your nutritional needs, and upload your own recipes to your cookbook, where you can find the nutritional information for each plate.

At Honest Kitchen we believe everyone should have access to know what's going in their body, so we disclose all available nutritional information in all our recipes, and we do our best to tell you what's in your food.

Recipes

How can I get the nutritional information for my own recipe?

To get your recipe's nutritional info, first, go to Add Recipe and fill out the recipe form, follow the requirements precisely (for example, list the ingredients with commas in between) for best results.

Then, head to My Cookbook, where you will find this recipe and all the ones you uploaded in the past. Click on the see full recipe button, and you will see the nutritional data for one serving.

Have fun!
